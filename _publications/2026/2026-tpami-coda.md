abstract: >-
  We propose CoDA, a convergent diffusion approximation framework for generating
  highly realistic face images with improved training stability and generation
  quality. By establishing a convergent approximation to the true data distribution
  through diffusion models, CoDA achieves superior fidelity while maintaining
  diversity, advancing the state of the art in face generation.
