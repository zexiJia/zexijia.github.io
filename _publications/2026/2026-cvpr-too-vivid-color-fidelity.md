abstract: >-
  Text-to-image models often produce oversaturated “too vivid” outputs when asked for
  realistic photos. We introduce a color fidelity dataset (CFD), a learned color fidelity
  metric (CFM), and a training-free refinement (CFR) that calibrates guidance to improve
  photographic color authenticity.
