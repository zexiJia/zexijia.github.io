abstract: >-
  We reinterpret classifier-free guidance as a geometric mismatch: Euclidean CFG pushes
  samples off the data manifold. MOG formulates guidance as a local optimal control problem
  and applies a closed-form Riemannian correction (with an Auto-MOG schedule) to reduce
  artifacts at high guidance without retraining.
