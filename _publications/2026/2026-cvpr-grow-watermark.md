abstract: >-
  We propose GROW, a training-free diffusion watermarking method that grows the mark
  across denoising steps via frequency-domain loss and gradient guidance, instead of
  one-shot embedding in initial noise. Extraction needs no DDIM inversion, so verification
  is roughly 100x faster than inversion-based baselines while keeping strong imperceptibility
  and robustness.
